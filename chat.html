Project Overview

This proposal covers the design, development, deployment and launch support of a custom Real Estate Operations CRM for UMANIE Homes Africa — a complete business management platform that goes far beyond a standard CRM.

The system is designed to manage the full lifecycle of your real estate and development operations: from construction project tracking, materials and labour costing, lead management, and property sales, through to invoicing, purchase agreement generation, client reviews, and eventually property management.

The first functional launch will be optimised for a lean team (CEO + Customer Service), with the architecture built to scale as you add roles like Project Manager, Sales Officer, Accountant, and Property Manager.

Not just a CRM. This is a full real estate development, sales, construction costing, document, review, hosting-supported, and property management operations platform — built specifically for UMANIE Homes Africa.

Submission format. This proposal may be shared as a live preview link and as a downloadable PDF for review, printing, internal sharing and signing.

Scope of Work

The following modules are included in this engagement:

📊 Dashboard

Full business overview: leads, active projects, properties, payments, follow-ups, and recent activities.

👥 Leads & Clients

Lead stages, follow-up history, site visit tracking, lead source, client type, and linked records.

🏗️ Projects & Properties

Construction project tracking, progress status, convert project to property/units, full project profiles.

📦 Project Inventory

Materials tracking with stock-in and stock-out, supplier records, remaining stock calculations.

👷 Workmanship / Labour

Worker/team payment tracking per project. Feeds into total project cost automatically.

🧾 Project Expenses

Other expenses: transport, permits, equipment, tools, etc. Full budget vs actual view.

📈 Sales Pipeline

From new enquiry to closed sale and handover. Reservations, discounts, and payment plans.

💰 Payments, Invoices & Receipts

Full payment tracking, overdue alerts, branded PDF invoices and receipts generation.

📄 Purchase Agreement Generator

Auto-populate agreements from client and property data. Preview, download, and print as PDF.

🗂️ Document Management

Organised document storage by client, project, property, and agreement type.

⭐ Customer Review System

Review alerts, digital form links, printable PDFs, approval workflow, and review history.

🏢 Property Management Module

Tenants, landlords, rent collection, maintenance requests, inspections, and management fees.

📋 Reports

Sales, payment, project cost, inventory, lead conversion, and staff activity reports.

⚙️ User Roles & Activity Logs

CEO, Customer Service, and future roles. Full audit trail of all actions in the system.

🌐 Deployment & Hosting Setup

System deployment, hosting setup, database configuration, SSL setup and launch preparation. Suitable free-tier cloud infrastructure may be used where usage allows.

Delivery Phases & Timeline

The project will be delivered as a focused 5-week functional launch, subject to timely feedback, prompt access to required information, and no major scope changes after sign-off. This timeline is designed to allow proper build quality, testing, deployment preparation, client review and launch support.

Phase 1
Foundation, Architecture & Core Setup
Week 1
  • Project setup, database architecture and authentication
  • User roles & permissions (CEO, Customer Service and future roles)
  • Activity logs & audit trail
  • Company profile, settings and base dashboard layout
  • Deployment environment and hosting setup preparation
Phase 2
CRM, Leads, Projects & Properties
Week 2
  • Leads & Clients module with stages, notes and follow-up history
  • Follow-ups & Site Visits
  • Projects module with construction status tracking
  • Properties / Units module
  • Convert Project → Property/Unit feature
  • Core dashboard data connection
Phase 3
Inventory, Labour, Expenses & Costing
Week 3
  • Project Inventory with stock-in and stock-out
  • Workmanship / Labour tracking
  • Project Expenses tracking
  • Project cost calculator and budget vs actual view
  • Mid-project working demo for review and feedback
Phase 4
Sales, Payments, Documents & Reviews
Week 4
  • Sales Pipeline module
  • Payment Plans & Installment tracking
  • Invoice and receipt generation (branded PDF)
  • Purchase Agreement Generator (PDF)
  • Document Management module
  • Customer Review System (digital link and printable form)
Phase 5
Property Management, Reports & Launch
Week 5
  • Property Management module foundation
  • Tenant, Landlord, Rent and Maintenance tracking
  • Reports module
  • Final QA, testing, deployment and launch readiness checks
  • Admin training session and handover support

Key Terms & Conditions

Functional Launch Timeline

Target delivery is 5 weeks for a functional launch, subject to timely feedback, prompt access to required content, and no major scope changes after sign-off.

Feedback Turnaround

Client feedback should be provided within 24–72 hours during development to maintain the 5-week launch timeline.

Hosting & Deployment

Cloud Usage Costs

If system usage grows beyond free-tier limits or premium services become necessary, additional third-party cloud, storage, email or hosting costs will be discussed and billed separately.

Custom Domain

A custom domain or subdomain can be configured when available. Domain purchase, renewal and any premium DNS/email/domain-related fees are billed separately unless otherwise agreed.

Support After Delivery

30 days of bug-fix support included post-launch. Ongoing support, backups, monitoring, minor improvements and hosting management available from GHS 1,000/month.

Development begins upon receipt of the initial payment (GHS 15,000). The final payment (GHS 10,000) is due upon completion and before final handover/live deployment.

UMANIE Homes Africa owns its business data, documents and final system usage rights upon full payment. Source code transfer terms can be agreed separately if required.

Scope Changes

Any new features added after sign-off will be scoped and quoted separately to protect the delivery timeline.
