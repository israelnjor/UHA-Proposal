Project Overview

Pribsons International proposes to design, develop, deploy and provide post-launch support for a custom Real Estate Operations CRM for UMANIE Homes Africa. This is not a generic CRM — it is a complete real estate development and operations platform built around the specific way UMANIE Homes works.

The system will manage the full journey: construction project tracking, materials and labour costing, lead management, property sales, invoicing, purchase agreement generation, customer reviews, and a future-ready property management module — all in one place, accessible by a small team today and scalable as the company grows.

Tech Stack: Built on Next.js (React), Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + File Storage), and Tailwind CSS — a modern, fast, and cost-efficient stack that supports free-tier hosting from day one, with clean pathways to scale.

Delivery format: This proposal may be shared as a live link for preview and as a downloadable PDF for review, internal sharing, and signing.

Scope of Work

The following system modules are included in this engagement. All modules, deployment, hosting setup, and initial post-launch support are covered within the agreed fee.

Frontend Next.js + React
Database + Auth Supabase (PostgreSQL)
File Storage Supabase Storage
Styling Tailwind CSS
PDF Generation React-PDF / Puppeteer
Hosting Vercel + Supabase
📊 Dashboard

Live business overview — leads, active projects, properties, pending payments, follow-ups, overdue alerts and recent activity feed.

👥 Leads & Clients

Full lead lifecycle management: stages, source tracking, follow-up history, site visits, and linked properties, invoices and agreements.

🏗️ Projects & Properties

Construction project tracking with status, progress, images, linked units and a Convert Project → Property / Units feature.

📦 Project Inventory

Materials tracking with stock-in and stock-out. Supplier records, purchase dates, remaining stock calculations, and receipt uploads.

👷 Workmanship & Labour

Worker and team payment records per project — daily, weekly, contract or milestone. Automatically feeds into the total project cost.

🧾 Project Expenses

Other project costs: transport, permits, equipment, tools, utilities. Full budget vs actual view and estimated profit calculation.

📈 Sales Pipeline

From new enquiry to closed sale and handover. Reservations, discounts, linked payment plans, agreements and sales officer assignment.

💰 Payments, Invoices & Receipts

Full payment tracking, instalment schedules, overdue alerts, and UMANIE-branded PDF invoice and receipt generation.

📄 Purchase Agreement Generator

Auto-populated purchase agreements from client and property records. Preview, download and print as branded PDF.

🗂️ Document Management

Organised file storage by client, project, property and document type — IDs, agreements, site plans, handover documents and more.

⭐ Customer Review System

Review alerts triggered after handover. Digital review form link and printable PDF form. CEO approval before publishing.

🏢 Property Management Module

Tenants, landlords, rent collection, maintenance requests, property inspections, and management fee tracking.

📋 Reports

Sales, payment, project cost, inventory, lead conversion, staff activity, and property management reports for the CEO.

⚙️ User Roles & Activity Logs

Role-based access (CEO, Customer Service, and future roles). Full audit trail — who created, edited, deleted or generated anything.

🌐 Deployment, Hosting & Launch Support

System deployment to Vercel and Supabase, SSL setup, domain configuration, database setup, and post-launch support. Free-tier infrastructure used where usage allows.

4-Week Delivery Plan

The system will be delivered as a focused 4-week functional launch. This timeline is achievable with the React + Supabase stack and requires timely feedback from the client — no longer than 24–48 hours per review checkpoint — and no major scope changes after Week 1 sign-off.

Week 1
Foundation, Architecture & Core Setup
Days 1–7
  • Project setup — Next.js, Supabase, Tailwind, repo structure
  • Database schema design for all modules
  • Authentication and session management
  • Role-based access control (CEO, Customer Service + future roles)
  • Activity log and audit trail system
  • Company profile and settings
  • Base dashboard layout with live data connections
  • Hosting environment setup (Vercel + Supabase)
Week 2
CRM, Projects, Properties & Costing
Days 8–14
  • Leads and Clients module (all stages, notes, follow-ups)
  • Site Visits tracking and management
  • Projects module with construction status and progress
  • Properties and Units module
  • Convert Project → Property / Units feature
  • Project Inventory — stock-in and stock-out
  • Workmanship and Labour tracking
  • Project Expenses and budget vs actual cost calculator
🔍 Mid-point demo — working system shown to client for review and feedback before proceeding.
Week 3
Sales, Payments, Documents & Reviews
Days 15–21
  • Sales Pipeline module (full stages)
  • Payment Plans and instalment tracking
  • Invoice generation with UMANIE branding (PDF)
  • Receipt generation with UMANIE branding (PDF)
  • Purchase Agreement Generator (auto-populated PDF)
  • Document Management module with file uploads
  • Customer Review System — digital link and printable form
  • Review approval workflow for CEO
Week 4
Property Management, Reports, QA & Launch
Days 22–28
  • Property Management module — tenants, landlords, rent and maintenance
  • Inspection records and management fee tracking
  • All reports module (sales, payments, project costs, activity)
  • Full QA and cross-module testing
  • Final deployment to live environment and domain configuration
  • CEO and team admin training session
  • Handover documentation and system walkthrough
🚀 Go live — system deployed and handed over. 30-day bug-fix support begins.

Key Terms & Conditions

4-Week Launch Conditions

The 4-week delivery target requires: no major scope changes after Week 1, client feedback within 24–48 hours at each checkpoint, and prompt provision of required content (logo, brand colours, legal wording etc.).

Revisions

Two revision rounds included within the agreed scope. Changes beyond the agreed scope or after Week 1 sign-off will be quoted separately to protect the timeline.

Hosting & Deployment

Deployment, hosting configuration, database setup, SSL and domain setup are included in this engagement. Vercel (frontend) and Supabase (database and storage) free tiers will be used where usage allows. Upgrades discussed as usage grows.

Ongoing Support (Post-Launch)

30 days of bug-fix support included after go-live. After that, ongoing hosting management, backups, monitoring and minor improvements are available from GHS 500/month.

Ownership & Data

UMANIE Homes Africa owns all its business data, documents and system usage rights upon completion of full payment. Source code transfer terms can be agreed separately if required.

Payment Terms

Development begins upon confirmed receipt of the GHS 15,000 initial payment. The final GHS 10,000 is due after client sign-off and before live deployment.

Legal Documents

The purchase agreement generator will produce structured PDFs based on approved company fields. Final legal wording must be reviewed and approved by UMANIE Homes Africa's legal representative before official use.

Third-Party Costs

If system usage exceeds free-tier limits or premium services are required, additional cloud, storage or email costs will be communicated transparently and billed separately.
